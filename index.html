Project Purpose

Revolutionizing Pizza Business

Biano's is a comprehensive mobile management solution designed specifically for pizza establishments. Our goal is to bridge the gap between complex industrial systems and small-to-medium enterprises, providing high-end analytics, inventory management, and a streamlined POS in the palm of your hand.

  • Efficiency: Reduce order processing time by 40%.
  • Accuracy: Real-time inventory tracking prevents stockouts.
  • Growth: Detailed analytics to identify best-selling products.
